Gameplay

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must fend off increasingly difficult waves of monsters. The longer you survive, the harder the challenge becomes, but the greater the rewards!

Collect experience orbs from defeated enemies to level up and choose powerful upgrades. Strategize your build to create the ultimate monster-slaying character and set new survival records.

Game Progression

  • • Begin with basic weapons and abilities
  • • Defeat enemies to earn experience and level up
  • • Choose from random upgrades at each level
  • • Survive against increasingly difficult waves
  • • Defeat bosses to unlock powerful items

Game Controls

How to Play

Movement

  • WASD or Arrow Keys - Move your character
  • Space - Dodge (if available)

Other Controls

  • ESC - Pause game
  • 1-6 - Use special abilities
  • Tab - View stats

Monster Survivors features automatic attacks - your character will attack the nearest enemies automatically with your selected weapons. Focus on movement and positioning to survive as long as possible!
