Game Description

"Adventures With Anxiety!" is an interactive story game created by Nicky Case that explores anxiety in an innovative way. In this game, you don't play as the person suffering from anxiety - you play as the anxiety itself! You'll make choices through dialogue to protect your human, while seeing how overprotection can sometimes lead to more problems. The game uses humor and thoughtful storytelling to help players understand the nature of anxiety and how to deal with it.

Controls

Mouse Click: Select dialogue options
Click Screen: Continue story
Click Options: Make decisions

Game Tips

  • Try different choices to see how they affect the story
  • Every decision affects your relationship with the human you're protecting
  • There are multiple endings depending on your choices
  • The game offers insightful perspectives on anxiety that might help understand this common emotion
