Game Description

"Six Cats Under" is a charming game developed by Team Bean Loop and Mosu that tells a bittersweet story. In this game, you've passed away, but your spirit can't rest because you're worried about the six cats you've left behind. As a ghost, you need to find ways to care for and protect these adorable felines, ensuring their well-being and safety. The game combines simulation and puzzle elements, allowing players to experience a touching story about life, responsibility, and love while taking care of cats.

Controls

Mouse Click: Select and interact
Drag and Drop: Move and place items
Click on Cats: Check their status
Click on Objects: Interact with environment

Game Tips

  • Each cat has a different personality and needs - understanding their traits will make the game easier
  • Pay attention to the cats' health and mood indicators to keep them satisfied
  • Explore various items in the environment; some may provide special help for cat care
  • New challenges will emerge as time passes, so stay vigilant
  • Interactions with other characters may reveal more of the game's backstory
